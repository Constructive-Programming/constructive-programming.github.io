Consulting

I help teams make their codebases correct by construction.

I'm Rodolfo Hansen — author of cats-eo, designer of the Fixed language, and the speaker behind Keep Your Types Small and Correct by Construction. I work primarily in Scala and Rust, with deep experience in typed functional design, optics, algebraic effects, and recursion schemes.

Engagements I take on:

  • IC contributions — ship features in Scala or Rust alongside your existing engineering team.
  • Architecture and advisory — review designs, narrow type signatures, and identify where capability-based abstractions or algebraic effects unlock simpler, safer code.
  • Optics, effects and recursion deep dives — bring cats-eo, Monocle, droste, or your effect system of choice to bear on knotty data-pipeline or state-management problems.
  • Workshops and training — adapted from the constructive programming workshops (logic, ADTs, streaming) for your team's stack and seniority.

Engagements range from a focused code review to a multi-month embedded role. If any of this sounds useful, I'd love to hear from you.
